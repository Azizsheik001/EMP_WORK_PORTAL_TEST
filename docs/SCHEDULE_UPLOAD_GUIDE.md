# Schedule Upload Guide - For Team Leads

## How to Upload Weekly Schedules

### Step 1: Download the Template

1. Log in to the AGS Workforce Portal
2. Go to the **Upload Schedules** tab
3. Select your **Client** from the dropdown
4. Select the **Week starting date** (pick the Monday of the week)
5. Click **Download Template CSV**

This gives you a CSV file pre-filled with your team's employee names and the correct dates.

### Step 2: Fill in the Schedule

Open the downloaded CSV in **Google Sheets** or **Excel** and fill in each cell:

| Employee Name | 2026-03-02 | 2026-03-03 | 2026-03-04 | 2026-03-05 | 2026-03-06 | 2026-03-07 | 2026-03-08 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sanjay Kumar G | 09:00-18:00 | 09:00-18:00 | 09:00-18:00 | 09:00-18:00 | 09:00-18:00 | OFF | OFF |
| Dhanya Sri | 06:00-14:00 | 06:00-14:00 | 06:00-14:00 | 06:00-14:00 | 06:00-14:00 | OFF | OFF |

### Shift Format Rules

- Use **24-hour format**: `HH:MM-HH:MM`
- Examples:
  - `09:00-18:00` = 9 AM to 6 PM
  - `09:00-17:00` = 9 AM to 5 PM
  - `08:00-17:00` = 8 AM to 5 PM
  - `06:00-14:00` = 6 AM to 2 PM
  - `14:00-22:00` = 2 PM to 10 PM
  - `22:00-06:00` = 10 PM to 6 AM (night shift)
- Use `OFF` for days off
- Leave cell empty for no assignment

### Common Shift Patterns

| Pattern | Description |
|---|---|
| `09:00-18:00` | Standard day shift (9h) |
| `09:00-17:00` | Standard day shift (8h) |
| `06:00-14:00` | Morning/First shift |
| `14:00-22:00` | Afternoon/Second shift |
| `22:00-06:00` | Night/Third shift |
| `OFF` | Day off |

### Step 3: Upload

1. Save the file as `.csv` (CSV format, not .xlsx)
2. Go back to **Upload Schedules** tab in the portal
3. Click **Choose File** and select your CSV
4. Click **Parse & Preview**
5. Review the schedule in the preview table
6. Check for any warnings (yellow boxes) — these show unmatched names or format issues
7. Click **Confirm & Save Schedule**

### Tips

- **Employee names must match exactly** as they appear in the system. The template pre-fills correct names for you.
- **One file per client per week.** Upload separately for each client.
- If you re-upload for the same week, it will **update** existing shifts (not duplicate them).
- You can also use the **Build Schedule** tab to create schedules directly in the portal without CSV.
